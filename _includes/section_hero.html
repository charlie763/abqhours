We all have a skill or service to share

Timebanking is a simple, social way to connect with others, swap and share skills and services, using time exchange instead of cash. For each hour you help someone in the TimeBank Albuquerque community, you earn one TimeDollar. You can then ‘spend’ that hour getting help from someone else in the TimeBank Albuquerque community and our global TimeBanking network.

Timebanking is open to individuals of all ages, abilities, employment status or mobility.
